Getting Started

Follow these quick steps to search for cards, manage your inventory, and keep your MTG collection organized.

1

Sign Up or Sign In

  1. Go to Sign Up and create an account with your email, username, and password.
  2. Already registered? Head to Sign In and log in with your credentials.
  3. Once logged in, the navigation bar will show your profile link and a sign-out button.
2

Search for Cards

  1. Navigate to the Card Search page.
  2. Use the filters (name, type, color identity, mana value, and commander legal toggle) to narrow your results.
  3. For example, to find all blue instant cards with mana value 3 or less: enter U in Color Identity, 3 in Mana Value <=, and type Instant in Type.
  4. Optionally, you can use the Commander Legal toggle to filter for cards that are legal in the Commander format.
  5. You can also search text using the Search Text field to find cards by name or other rules text.
  6. Click Search to fetch matching cards, then browse with the Prev/Next buttons if there are multiple pages.
3

Add Cards to Your Inventory

  1. From the search results, pick a card you own or want to track.
  2. Click Add to Inventory. If you aren't signed in, you'll be prompted to do so first.
  3. A success toast confirms the card was saved. Repeat for each card you want to store.
  4. You can add multiple copies of the same card by clicking Add to Inventory again; the quantity will increase accordingly.
4

View and Manage Your Inventory

  1. Open the My Stuff page from the navigation bar.
  2. Review the list of saved cards, quantities, and recent updates.
  3. Use your browser's refresh or repeat the search/add flow to keep the list current.
  4. To remove a card, click Remove. This decreases the quantity by one or removes it entirely if only one copy existed.
5

Delete Your Account

  1. Visit your Profile page while signed in.
  2. Click Delete Account and confirm the prompt. This permanently removes your profile and inventory data.
  3. After deletion, you'll be redirected to the homepage. You can create a new account anytime.
